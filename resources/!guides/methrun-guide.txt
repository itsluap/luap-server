Methrun Guide:

1. methrun npc is at postal 9249
2. you need 20 bags of meth to start a run, once you have 5 bags you can select hand goodies
3. once you hand over 20 bags of meth you can select check goods 
4. from here you will get a task to pick up an armored car
5. steal the armored car and go to the drop off location, enemies will be waiting for you
6. find the npc and grab the items from him
7. go to the drop off location and leave the car along with the items in the trunk
8. after leaving the area you can head back to the boss and get your payment
